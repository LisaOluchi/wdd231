MIG - Site Plan

Site Name

MIG Chamber Of Comerece

This name represents a site that focuses on networking business.

Site Purpose

This website will serve as an online platform for a local Chamber of Commerce, focusing on promoting business interests and fostering community engagement..

Scenarios

  • How can I grow my business and get training too?
  • I want to start a new brandline where can I promote it?

Color Schema

  • #f7d700 - Yellow: Used for headings and accents.
  • #ffffff - White: Used for backgrounds and highlights.
  • #000000 - Black: Used for body text.
  • #001f3f - Navy: Used for the header background.
  • #f7d700 - Yellow: Used for image backgrounds.

Typography

  • Header Font: 'Arial', - Used for main headings.
  • Body Font: 'sans-serif', - Used for body text and paragraphs.
